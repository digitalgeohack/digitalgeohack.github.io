Digital Geoscience Hackathon

DigitalGeoHack

A hackathon in Paris focusing on geoscience and datascience. With funding from the EGAE, the Digital Geosciences Hackathon will bring together computer scientists, machine learning specialists and geoscience experts to explore new ideas for digitalization and automation in geophysical research, exploration and industrial production processes. You don’t need to code to come, all levels of experience are welcome.

When: Friday 15th and Saturday 16th November 2019.

Where: Institut de Physique du Globe de Paris.
